WHAT IS JAHCARA®

JAHCARA® is a vietnamese clothing company. The letters J, A and H stand for the three owner of the company, Jammy, Alvin and Henry. The letter C in our logo stands for the word "cara", which is also a part of our brand name. It stands for the Cara from our brand name, Jahcara, the meanings of cara is the sound that it made, cara sounds like the work karat, which is the measurement of how pure gold is. We change karat to cara because us vietnamese people usually read it as cara rather than karat and we are a Vietnamese company and we want people to know that we’re proud Vietnamese and we’re proud of our company, our brand coming from Vietnam, our home

Our target audience are wealthy people and people that are well paid. People that enjoy simple clothing that can pair well with everyone and helps fit in with the youth nowadays while still showing that you’re wearing a luxurious clothing brand product. Anyone regardless of their age can use and enjoy our products but the age that we targetted and also are most likely to use our product are from young adults, teenagers to people at 30 - 35 years old.

ABOUT OUR PRODUCTS

JAHCARA® creates products that embody modern luxury through simplicity, quality, and intention. Each piece is designed with clean silhouettes, premium materials, and subtle detailing that favor elegance over excess. Rather than chasing trends, the brand focuses on timeless appeal and offering clothing and accessories that feel refined, durable, and versatile for any setting. Every item reflects a commitment to craftsmanship and identity, allowing the wearer to express confidence without loud branding or unnecessary embellishment. JAHCARA® stands for sophistication you can see, feel, and live in.

Our brand is made for everyone, prices are reasonable for a luxury brand. Wearing our clothes, you'll feel comfort and safe while feeling proud. You'll feel that your money have been well spent into something that you can wear for a really long period of time. Wearing our products will help you fit into any group, unlike Shift Society products.
